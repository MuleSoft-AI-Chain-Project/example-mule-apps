How MFA with Email OTP Works:

  • First, you authenticate with your username and password.
  • If your credentials are correct, a one-time password (OTP) is sent to your registered email address.
  • You are then prompted to enter the OTP to complete the authentication process.
  • This provides an extra layer of security by requiring you to have access to your email account.
